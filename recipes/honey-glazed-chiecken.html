Honey Glazed Chicken

Description

Welcome to the world of flavor with our tantalizing Honey Glazed Chicken! Juicy chicken thighs slathered in a luscious honey glaze, creating a harmony of sweet and savory that will leave your taste buds begging for more. Get ready to elevate your dinner game with this easy yet delicious recipe!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Start by gathering all your ingredients.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes until well combined. Set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until it's lightly browned, approximately 5 minutes.
  5. Once the chicken is browned, pour the honey mixture into the skillet.
  6. Continue cooking and stirring until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened, about 5 more minutes.
  7. Once everything is cooked to perfection, serve hot and savor the deliciousness!
