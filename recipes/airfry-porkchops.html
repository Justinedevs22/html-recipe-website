Air Fried Pork Chops

Description

Introducing our Air Fried Pork Chops recipe - a delightful twist on a classic favorite! Prepare to be amazed as tender pork chops are transformed into crispy perfection with the magic of air frying. Say goodbye to greasy splatters and hello to juicy, flavorful pork chops with a golden brown crust. With minimal effort and maximum flavor, this recipe is sure to become a new weeknight favorite for you and your family!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 (5 ounce) boneless pork chops
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 380 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a flat shallow dish, combine Parmesan cheese, paprika, garlic powder, salt, parsley, and pepper, ensuring they are mixed well.
  3. Coat each pork chop with olive oil, then dredge both sides of each chop in the Parmesan mixture. Place them on a plate.
  4. Once the air fryer is preheated, place 2 pork chops in the basket.
  5. Cook for 10 minutes, flipping halfway through the cooking time for even crispiness.
  6. Transfer the cooked pork chops to a cutting board and let them rest for 5 minutes.
  7. Repeat the cooking process with the remaining pork chops.
  8. Serve hot and savor the crispy goodness of your air fried pork chops!
